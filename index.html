Mobile Application
MED
REACH
MedReach is a rural telemedicine mobile app designed to
connect patients in underserved areas of Sri Lanka with
doctors, pharmacists, and health services through a
simple, Android-based platform.
Powered by Flutter and Firebase, it ensures accessible,
secure, and real-time healthcare anytime, anywhere.
